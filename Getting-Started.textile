In the beginning, there existed two deities known as Uranus and Gaia. They gave birth to "Saturn":http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saturn_(mythology), god of time, who set reality in motion. Ultimately, time yielded the existence of the sky, the sea, and the end of life---death. To rule these notions, Saturn had three sons: "Jupiter":http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jupiter_(mythology) (sky), "Neptune":http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Neptune_(mythology) (sea), and "Pluto":http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pluto_(mythology) (underworld).
